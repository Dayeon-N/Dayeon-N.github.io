CHAPTER 8.1: PHYSICAL SECURITY

Health aspects

Repetitive Strain Injury (RSI)

- When joints such as the shoulder, wrists, or fingers swell, causing pain
- Damage gets worse if left untreated
- Can lead to hand/arm immobility

Causes

  • Typing for an extended period of time
  • Typing with incorrect posture
  • Clicking on mouse rapidly/extendedly
  • Holding mouse incorrectly
  • Working in a cramped space

Ways to prevent

  • Take periodic breaks
  • Use ergonomic keyboard & mouse
  • Use a wrist rest
  • Ensure sufficient space for comfortable work

Back problems

Causes

  • Not sitting upright while working
  • Sitting with poor posture
  • Working in a cramped space
  • Incorrect computer screen positioning

Ways to prevent

  • Take periodic breaks
  • Use adjustable chairs with good back support
  • Sit upright with good posture
  • Keep feet flat on the floor

Eye problems

Causes

  • Staring at the computer for extended periods of time
  • Working with poor room lighting
  • Glare/flicker of the computer screen
  • Dirt on screen can cause eye strain

Ways to prevent

  • Take periodic breaks
  • Use TFT rather than CRT
  • Use anti-glare screens
  • Ensure good rooms lighting
  • Clean dirt

Headaches

Causes

  • Incorrect lighting
  • Screen reflections
  • Flickering screens

Ways to prevent

  • Use anti-glare screen/window blinds to cut out reflections
  • Take regular breaks and exercise
  • Test eyes regularly and use middle vision glasses if necessary

Ozone irritation

Causes

  • Laser printers in an office area due to dry skin and respiratory problems

Ways to prevent

  • Proper ventilation to remove the ozone gas as quickly as possible
  • Laser printers housed in a designated printer room
  • Change to other types of the printer if necessary
